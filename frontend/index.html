How Support Hub fits into your day

From new requests to weekly reports, Support Hub keeps your team aligned. Each step is structured so nothing gets lost, and AI handles the repetitive work.

1. Capture every request

New tickets raised from forms or internal tools land in a single queue with full context.

2. AI suggests category & priority

The model analyzes the description to propose a category and urgency while you review.

3. Teams work from a shared workspace

Agents see ownership, status, and history so they can follow through without switching tools.

4. Measure and improve

Analytics show patterns across queues, helping you improve SLAs and staffing.
